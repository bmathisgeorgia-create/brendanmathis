About Brendan

Passionate about healthcare.

I serve as the primary Special Assistant to Commissioner John F. King. I provide comprehensive support to the Commissioner across legislative, administrative, and constituent affairs, acting as a key liaison to ensure seamless coordination of departmental priorities. My legislative responsibilities include engaging with the Georgia General Assembly to advocate for policies that enhance insurance regulation, consumer protections, and fire safety standards.

I work closely with Georgia Access for Business (GAFB), focusing on the implementation of Individual Coverage Health Reimbursement Arrangements (ICHRA) and Qualified Small Employer Health Reimbursement Arrangements (QSEHRA). I ensure successful project execution by collaborating with insurers, brokers, and employers to expand access to affordable health coverage for small businesses and individuals throughout Georgia.
